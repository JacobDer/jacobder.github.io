US Forestry Map

June 27, 2020
Jacob Der

For the nature lover (or the nature hater) thinking of moving somewhere in the continental US, here is a graphical interface to tell you about the forestry and wooded areas of different states. The numbers given for each state are in acres.

Also keep in mind that these numbers should be considered relative to the size of the state to give an accurate idea of density.

The data used to make this map is available here.
