Political Philosophy Resources

June 20, 2020
Jacob Der

Here I want to compile some resources for political philosophy. In particular, a goal of mine is to dispel some myth and misunderstanding of Marxism and related philosophy. Feel free to let me know about anything that I might add.

General

Locke's Political Philosophy: A nice description of Locke's thoughts on the state of nature, property, and the purpose of government.

Marxism

Capital Volume I: A dense, almost scientific work by Marx. It contains rigorous arguments and critiques that, while sometimes dry, are worth reading.
A People's History of the United States: An alternate narrative for US history, focused more on the working class.
