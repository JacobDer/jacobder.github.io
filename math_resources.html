Math Resources

June 20, 2020
Jacob Der

Here I want to compile some resources for any math or math-related things that I think are interesting or relevant. Feel free to let me know about anything that I might add.

Combinatorics

Enumerative Combinatorics: A good book that covers a ton of material, and has more exercises than you could ever want.
Online Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences: A fun/useful website that acts as a database of integer sequences. It's a good tool for referencing sequences you may encounter, as well as something to contribute to in case you find a sequence that isn't catalogued.
